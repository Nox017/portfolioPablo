About Me

Something about me and my passion

Hi, I’m Pablo. I am an associate's degree student in 3D Animations, Games and Interactive Environments. I enjoy living in an environment where learning and acquiring new skills is the norm, in order to be prepared in the best way to partake in this great sector’s labor market. Most of my skills were self taught, so in order to offer the best quality and optimal work, I demand nothing but the best from myself. For me, the most enticing and interesting part of this line of work is game design, 3D modeling, sound design, post production and special effects. I am looking forward to learning the ropes in this industry to improve my own skills.
